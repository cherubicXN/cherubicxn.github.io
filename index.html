I finished my Ph.D. at Wuhan University, advised by Prof. Liangpei Zhang and Gui-Song Xia in CAPTAIN group. Currently, I am working with Prof. Tianfu Wu at NC State University as a visiting scholar. My research interests include line segment detection and its applications in 3D computer vision.
