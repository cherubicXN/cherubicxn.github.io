Biography

I am currently a PhD candidate in Wuhan University majoring 3D computer vision under the supervision with Prof. Liangpei Zhang and Gui-Song Xia in the CAPTAIN group of LIESMARS (State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing) of Wuhan University. Before starting my PhD, I received a Bachelor degree in Computational Mathematics at Wuhan University in Jun. 2014. From Sep. 2014 to Jun. 2016, I have worked as a master student in LIESMARS with Prof. Gui-Song Xia on structure-from-motion (SfM) and visual correspondence. Currently, I’m continuing my studying toward a Ph.D degree on 3D reconstruction.
